About GreenEarn

GreenEarn is an innovative platform that leverages artificial intelligence to make plastic bottle recycling smarter and more efficient. Our mission is to encourage sustainable habits by rewarding users for recycling plastic bottles using our AI-powered detection system.

Our Vision

We envision a cleaner, greener planet where technology and community work together to reduce plastic waste. By making recycling easy, fun, and rewarding, we aim to inspire positive environmental change.

How It Works

  • Detect plastic bottles using your device's camera.
  • Earn points for every bottle you recycle.
  • Redeem your points for rewards or contribute to environmental projects.

Join Us

Be a part of the GreenEarn community and help us make a real difference. Every bottle counts!
